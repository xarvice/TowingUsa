Frequently Asked Questions

How long will it take for a tow truck to arrive?

Our average response time is 30 minutes or less in most service areas. During peak hours or severe weather conditions, wait times may be slightly longer.

What types of vehicles can you tow?

We can tow all types of passenger vehicles including sedans, SUVs, luxury cars, and sports cars. Our flatbed trucks can accommodate vehicles of various sizes safely.

Do you provide insurance during towing?

Yes, we are fully insured and licensed. Your vehicle is covered under our comprehensive insurance policy during the entire towing process.
